No Need for Books

No Need For Books is a game where you have to understand the needs of the character and help him to move forward with his life.

This game was developed in 48 hours for the 5º edition of 99Jam with theme "Transformative Education".

  • Role: Programming and Pixel Art.
  • Tools: Unity, C#, Aseprite.
  • Links: Itchio, Cartrdge

ALZH

ALZH is a mobile exploration game using the accelerometer as input along with a simple mechanics, where you are a luminous character and have to find the exit of the labyrinth, but for this, you have to go through some obstacles being the biggest of them the environment dark.

The game was made at Cafundó Academy #5, a creative immersion program within the Cafundó Estúdio Criativo, aimed at qualifying and delivering market experience for those selected.

  • Role: Programming.
  • Tools: Unity, C#.
  • Links: Google Play Store

Escape to Heaven

Escape to Heaven's a puzzle isometric game about a guy who needs to climb the stairs to paradise. All your bad decisions will be taken in consideration on your final judge. Be fast and remember, god will be watching you!

This game was developed in 72 hours for the DreamHack Jam with theme "Apocalypse", being one of the 5 winners and being displayed at the event "DreamHack Denver 2017".

  • Role: Programming.
  • Tools: Unity, C#.
  • Links: Game Jolt | Behance
